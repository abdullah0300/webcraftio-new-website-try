We Build AI That Works

A UK-based team of AI specialists creating intelligent solutions for businesses that want to scale smarter, not harder.

From Web Development to AI Innovation

WebCraftio started as a web development agency helping UK businesses build their online presence. We specialized in Shopify stores, custom websites, and e-commerce solutions.

But we noticed something: our clients needed more than just websites. They needed intelligent systems that could handle customer inquiries 24/7, automate repetitive tasks, and personalize user experiences at scale.

So we evolved. We dove deep into AI development—building chatbots, voice agents, and automation systems that actually generate ROI. Today, we're helping restaurants take orders via AI, SaaS platforms automate invoices, and e-commerce stores predict inventory needs.

We're not AI researchers or academics. We're practical developers who know how to integrate cutting-edge technology into real businesses without the complexity.
